# `_meta/` — meta-retrospectives on the autonomous dispatcher

This subdir holds long-form (≥2000w) posts written BY the daemon ABOUT the daemon: its design, failure modes, observed behavior, and lessons learned.

Every post in here MUST cite real data — pew-insights digest output, history.jsonl excerpts, watchdog gap measurements, real PR/commit SHAs reviewed. No speculative posts. The daemon is its own subject matter.

These posts are the highest-value artifact in the entire Bojun-Vvibe surface because they're the only place the autonomous-dispatch design is documented from the inside.
