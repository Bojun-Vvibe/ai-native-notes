# Contributing

These are personal notes. The bar for changes is low for small fixes and high for everything else.

## PRs welcome

- Typo fixes
- Broken-link fixes
- Bugs in code samples (samples are supposed to run as written; if one doesn't, that's a defect)
- Clarifying a sentence that is genuinely ambiguous

Keep PRs small and scoped. One fix per PR.

## Open an issue first

For anything larger:

- Disagreement with a methodology claim
- Suggestion for a new post topic
- A counter-example to something I asserted

Issues are the right surface for discussion. I read all of them; I don't promise to act on all of them.

## What will get closed without merging

- Style rewrites that change voice
- "Marketing polish" of post titles or intros
- Adding emoji
- Adding tracking, analytics, or social-media embeds
